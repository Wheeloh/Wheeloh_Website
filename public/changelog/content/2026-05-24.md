# Wheeloh v1.0.1 — The Intelligence & Performance Update
*24/05/2026*

Welcome to **Wheeloh 1.0.1**! This version introduces our brand new gallery synchronization, detailed technical specifications for your spots, and a revamped points system with tuning bonuses. Here’s everything that’s new:

---

## Intelligent Gallery Sync

- **Scan your entire photo library**: Import all your past car spots in one tap. The app automatically scans your gallery to detect cars, saving you hours of manual selection.
- **Anti-cheat camera check**: Only authentic photos taken by your device's camera are accepted. Screenshots and web downloads are automatically detected and filtered out.
- **Smart duplicates grouping**: If you took multiple photos of the same car or at a car meet, the app automatically groups them together to keep your garage and feed clean.
- **Instant rescan cache**: Running the sync again is instant! Previously scanned photos are skipped automatically, saving your time and battery.

---

## Technical Specifications (Car Specs)

- **Detailed technical sheets**: Every spot now displays full tech specs: engine layout, horsepower, torque, weight, 0-100 km/h acceleration, top speed, estimated price, and rarity.
- **Smart specifications fallback**: For rare or custom cars not yet in our database, our AI automatically estimates the correct specs so you always have a complete sheet.

---

## Points & Tuning Bonus

- **Category-based points**: Earn more points for spotting rare cars (like supercars, classic rally cars, or iconic models) compared to daily drivers.
- **Tuning bonus (+25%)**: Spotted a modified car? Get a +25% point bonus if the app detects visible modifications like custom body kits, aftermarket wheels, or sports exhausts.

---

## App Performance & Comfort

- **Lag-free uploads**: Upload your high-resolution photos without the app freezing. The image compression engine has been optimized to run completely in the background.
- **Original quality backup**: A copy of your original photo in its full, uncompressed format is preserved in your archive storage.
- **Refreshed Likes & Design**: Enjoy a brand new interactive slider for liking posts, along with minor camera UI enhancements.
- **Improved Apple Login**: Smoother and more reliable sign-in flow for Apple users.
